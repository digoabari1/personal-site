About

Who Is David Abari?

I’m a fashion enthusiast turned software Engineer with a passion for Front-End Web Development.

At a very young age, I fell in love with fashion and I became very passionate about my appearance. I was always the kid who put on different outfits that made you think twice. Over time, I also became passionate about music and I became interested in building a music streaming platform.

This new curiosity led me into the world of web development. I started watching tutorials on youtube and slowly started learning the intricacies of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Excited about my newly acquired skills, I began building small personal projects as well as websites for family and friends. I really loved the whole web-development process and at this stage, it became apparent to me that this is a career path I would love to go down.

I decided to hone in on my craft and become a well-rounded full-blown Front-End Web Developer. I completed the Scrimba Front-End Developer Career Path and I also learned several front-end frameworks and libraries while studying Computer Science at Georgia State University.
